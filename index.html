COMFIESTA 2K23

Comfiesta 2k23 promises to be an exceptional symposium, offering a platform for learning and Enjoying in the field of computer science. Hosted by "the Department of Computer Science at Rajiv Gandhi College of Engineering and technology", this event is a must-attend for students, and professionals passionate about technology and its ever-evolving landscape. Make sure to mark your calendar for September 22, 2023, and join us for a day filled with knowledge, inspiration, and fun at Comfiesta 2k23!

Events

The college symposium will provide a diverse range of opportunities for students to learn, showcase their talents, and engage in friendly competition. It's a platform that encourages both technical and non-technical skill development and fosters creativity and collaboration among the student community. Technical and non-Technical events are listed below.

Technical Events

code Debugging

Debugging is a crucial part of software development. It involves identifying and fixing errors or bugs in our code to make it work correctly. Debugging is often a systematic process, and it may take some time to locate and fix the issue. Patience and persistence are key.

Technical quiz

Technical quiz is designed to test your knowledge of various technical topics, from computer science and programming to networking and web development. Whether you're a seasoned tech professional or just getting started on your technical journey, this quiz will challenge your understanding and may even teach you something new.

Register

Technical connections

In today's interconnected world, understanding how different technologies and systems communicate and work together is essential. Whether you're an experienced or just curious about the technical underpinnings of our interconnected world, we hope this session will provide valuable insights and deepen your understanding of the digital ecosystem.

Non-Technical Events

Free Fire

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled battleground of Free Fire! Free Fire is a popular battle royale mobile game developed by 111 Dots Studio and published by Garena. With millions of players worldwide, it has become one of the most exciting and accessible battle royale games available on mobile platforms.

BGMI

BGMI is a highly anticipated mobile battle royale game developed and published by Krafton, Inc. BGMI offers a thrilling and immersive battle royale experience designed specifically for mobile devices. If you enjoy fast-paced battle royale action on your mobile device, BGMI is definitely worthy!

Mini Militia

Mini Militia, also known as "Doodle Army 2," is a popular 2D multiplayer shooter game for mobile devices. It was developed by Appsomniacs LLC and has gained a significant following for its fast-paced, action- packed gameplay that brings intense combat to the palm of your hand.
